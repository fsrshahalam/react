How are Impact Points (RPI) calculated?

Every completed review earns a base of 20 points, multiplied by a Speed Factor:

  • • 1.5x (30 pts) — Lightning Fast (≤ 3 days)
  • • 1.0x (20 pts) — On-Time (4-5 days)
  • • 0.7x (14 pts) — Delayed (6-7 days)
  • • 0.4x (8 pts) — Overdue (8+ days)

Achievement Tiers: Rising Star (30+), Expert (60+), Elite (100+), and Legend (200+).
